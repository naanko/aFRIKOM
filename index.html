About Us

Afrikom was established in 2008 and registered with cooperate affairs commission (CAC) in 2018 as a micro enterprise in human resources development and services. The centre is registered with Small and Medium Enterprise Development Agency of Nigeria (SMEDAN) and also a member of Nigeria Association of Small-Scale Industries (NASSI) and Jos Chamber of Commerce, Industry, Mines and Agriculture (JOCCIMA) respectively.

Training that we do

Our training programmes are focus on range of creative techniques and leadership behaviours that unleash creative potential of our participants.

Graphic Design

Participants will learn how to combine images and text to solve visual problems using CorelDraw, Photoshop, Canva and other packages.

Web Design

In the training, participants will get the opportunity to use visual studio and other text editors and programmes to create appealing, aesthetic, and interactive web pages using various graphic items, such as logos, illustration and so more as per client requirement.

Digital Marketing

There are many ways to promote a business online. Participants will learn how to familiarize themselves with how these digital channels work, which options are best suited to them, and how to set up a clear plan or strategy that will help them achieve their goals and access their results.

Computer Coaching4kids

Children will have the opportunity to learn about computers and become "computer literate." CC4C will better prepare children for the fast-changing information society. It will build a general awareness of the ranges of tasks that computers can do and how computers are used to solve daily-life problems, and to what extent computers affect individuals and society.

Computer Applications

Participants will get the opportunity to have hands-on exercise on computer applications such as Microsoft Office and Statistical Packages.

Trade Skills

Learning a trade involves acquiring specialized knowledge and practical skills that open the doors to job creation after training. Participants will have opportunities available to learn various trade skills.
